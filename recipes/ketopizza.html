Keto Pizza

The recipe I am sharing with you today is a newer and more non-traditional Keto Pizza recipe that I created last year while I was on a Keto diet. I used this recipe several times for family get-togethers and other family style dinners with my own family.
    List of Ingredients
      8 eggs
      2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
      1 lb cooked Italian sausage
      1 cup pizza sauce
      1 cup shredded pizza blend cheese
      1 cup cooked bacon - crumbled
